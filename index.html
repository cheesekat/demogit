Top Three:
1. For hungry people who are indecisive, The Food Wheel, is a web page that lets you input your favorite restaurants and it displays them around a spinner, then you tap the spinner to spin the arrow, choosing the restaurant for you. Unlike GrubHub, our product makes deciding fun.
